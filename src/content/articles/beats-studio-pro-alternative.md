## Why Beats Studio Pro Command Premium Prices

**Beats Studio Pro** is Apple's flagship over-ear — USB-C lossless, spatial audio, and ANC tuned for iPhone users who want the Beats look in the studio and on flights. At **$320–380**, you're paying for brand, ecosystem, and polish.

For Android users or anyone who doesn't need Apple Music lossless, the value proposition thins fast.

## Why Soundcore Space One Is Our Pick

We already featured **Soundcore Space One** in our AirPods Max alternative — they remain one of Amazon's best over-ear values. Hybrid ANC, LDAC support, 40-hour battery, and a foldable design under $100 make them the obvious **Beats Studio Pro alternative** for budget shoppers.

You won't get Beats branding or Apple's spatial tricks. You will get comfortable daily headphones that don't require a credit card limit increase.

### Who this is for

- Commuters who want over-ear ANC on a budget
- Students and WFH workers in open offices
- Anyone who liked Beats aesthetics but not the price

### Who should skip

- Apple ecosystem power users who want seamless handoff
- Audiophiles chasing reference-grade sound
- You need the tightest possible ANC on airplanes

### Pros

- Strong value under $100
- LDAC and app EQ
- Long battery life
- Comfortable for multi-hour wear

### Cons

- Plastic build vs Beats premium feel
- ANC not class-leading
- No lossless over Lightning/USB-C Apple path
