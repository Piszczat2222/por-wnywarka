## Home Grooming Saves Hundreds Per Year

Professional grooming runs $50–100+ per visit depending on breed and location. With the right tools from Amazon, most owners can handle brushing, bathing, ear cleaning, and basic maintenance between salon visits — or skip the salon entirely for short-coated breeds.

We selected tools with **high Amazon review counts** and recommendations from groomers and veterinarians. This isn't about fancy gadgets — it's about the core kit that keeps your dog clean, comfortable, and mat-free.

### Grooming frequency guide

- **Short coats:** slicker brush weekly, bath monthly
- **Long/double coats:** brush 3–4x weekly, dematting comb as needed
- **All breeds:** ear cleaning weekly, teeth brushing daily if possible

Start with the slicker brush and shampoo if you're on a budget. Add the bath brush sprayer and grooming table when you're ready to level up home baths.
