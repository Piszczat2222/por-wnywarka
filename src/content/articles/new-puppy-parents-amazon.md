## The First 30 Days With a Puppy Are Chaos, These Help

Bringing home a puppy is equal parts joy and panic. Accidents on the carpet, chewed phone chargers, 3 AM whining, it's all normal, but the right gear makes the learning curve survivable. We built this list from what experienced dog owners and trainers actually recommend on Amazon, not random impulse buys.

Every item here addresses a **specific new-puppy problem**: crate comfort, potty accidents, teething destruction, leash pulling, and separation anxiety. None of these are luxury splurges, they're the practical starter kit that saves your floors, furniture, and sanity.

### What to buy first

If you're shopping in stages, prioritize the **enzymatic cleaner** (accidents will happen), **crate pad**, and **teething chew toy**. Add the harness and potty bells once you're ready for structured outdoor training. The heartbeat plush is optional but worth it if crate training is rough.
