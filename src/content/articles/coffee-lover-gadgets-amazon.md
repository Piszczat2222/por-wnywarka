## Stop Paying $6 for Lattes — Build the Bar at Home

Home coffee rabbit holes go deep: $2,000 espresso machines, custom grinders, water recipes. You don't need any of that to make **dramatically better coffee** than a drip machine or pod system. These Amazon gadgets target the highest-impact upgrades in order.

The formula is simple: **fresh ground beans + weighed water + right temperature = café-quality cup**. Everything on this list serves one of those three variables.

### Upgrade path (in order)

1. Burr grinder (biggest flavor jump)
2. Digital scale (~$15 Amazon basics works too)
3. Pour-over dripper or immersion brewer
4. Gooseneck kettle when you're ready for precision
